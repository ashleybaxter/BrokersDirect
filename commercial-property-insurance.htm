Commercial property insurance from Brokers Direct includes commercial and residential combined. Got a portfolio of commercial let property and looking for low cost insurance? Brokers Direct provide such cover.

What Does This Policy Cover?

Our policy offers comprehensive cover for most commercial properties including:

  • Offices, including taxi offices, travel agents, and estate agents.
  • Hairdressers and beauticians.
  • Restaurants, including cafés and high risk eateries, such as fast food takeaways.
  • Dental surgeries.
  • Car accessory stores.
  • Furniture stores.
  • Garden centres.
  • Commercial & residential combined.

Vacant And Unoccupied Commercial Property Insurance

We provide Unoccupied Commercial Property Insurance. This will provide you with cover for standard perils and property owner's liability

Residential And Commercial Combined

Insurance cover is also available for 'Commercial & Residential' combined policies throughout the UK. This policy covers buildings with a combination of residential and commercial interests such as offices, shops, restaurants etc with residential accommodation attached. This includes commercial policies where the landlord lives above. We also provide cover for multiple tenancy types.
