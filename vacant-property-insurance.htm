Vacant Property Insurance

As the name implies, this insurance provides cover for property that is unoccupied. It is mainly used where property is undergoing modernisation but also applies to property that is being offered for sale.

For this specific type of cover we unfortunately cannot offer quotes instantly online. To speak to one of our Front Line Advisers about obtaining a quote for vacant property insurance, call 0800 311 2023.

The call centre will be open from 8:00am - 5:30pm Monday to Friday, and 9:00am - 5:00pm on Saturday. However, there is an answer machine service where you can leave a message if you call out of hours.
